# Section copy refinement

Sharpen words already on the page. Goal: every line carries information.

Pass criteria:
- Front-load the point; cut throat-clearing intros.
- Replace buzzwords (passionate, leverage, synergy, ninja, rockstar, results-driven) with concrete claims.
- Prefer strong verbs and short sentences. Active voice.
- "Show, don't claim": convert assertions into evidence where possible.
- No waving-emoji greetings, no filler.
- Keep voice consistent with existing copy.

Process:
1. Quote the current line(s).
2. Give the tightened version.
3. For high-stakes lines (hero, positioning) offer 1–2 alternates; elsewhere commit to one.
4. Apply edits in the `content/` files.

For larger rewrites, use the `content-editor` agent.
